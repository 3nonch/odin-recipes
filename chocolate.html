How to Store and Freeze Fudge

Store the fudge in an airtight container (with wax paper between layers) at room temperature for one to two weeks. There's no need to refrigerate fudge. To freeze fudge, wrap it tightly in wax paper, then wrap it again in aluminum foil. Place the foil-wrapped fudge in a freezer-safe container or wrap it in another layer of foil. Freeze for up to three months. Thaw in the refrigerator overnight.
